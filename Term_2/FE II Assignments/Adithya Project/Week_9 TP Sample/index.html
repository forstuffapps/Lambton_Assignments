Cutting boards that never let you down

The purposefully basic patterns of our distinctive Crate & Barrel cutting boards focus all emphasis on the natural beauty of wood. These face-grain maple boards with a whitewash finish highlight the wood's milky tones and faint texture.

The carved groove catches juices and crumbs as you cut, and the rounded edges provide a soothing contrast to the crisp lines of the boards. The carving board also has juice wells for a nice presentation.
