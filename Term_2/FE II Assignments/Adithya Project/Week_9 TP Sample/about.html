About Kitchen Works

We are a Super High Density Polyethylene Plastic Cutting Boards Direct Manufacturer/Distributor. We serve the restaurant industry, wholesale distributors, chain stores, numerous government entities, and homeowners from our Sarnia warehouse. We ship to all 50 states as well as the majority of overseas nations.

Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service. We only sell cutting boards and cutting board materials. We are not just a smallwares company that sells cutting boards and personalised cutting boards.

For government organisations, wholesalers, and high volume consumers, we have discount programmes in place; please call for a bid price.

Whether you need one of our standard cutting boards, custom cutting boards, or are interested in Wholesale or Special Manufacturing, we are here to meet your needs. Our quality is unsurpassed, our prices are low, and our customer service is the best around.

We also can produce custom cutting boards with special designs, shapes, grooves, and even company logos. We have a state-of-the-art CNC machine that allows us to make precise designs using AutoCad. No project is too small for our team. If you have a special request, please contact us.
