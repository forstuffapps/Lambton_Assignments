About Us

Hillier Fryers Ltd. was founded in the early 1900s by John Hillier. At the time, John was a young engineer who was passionate about cooking. He developed a deep fryer that was made entirely of stainless steel and had oil filtration to ensure that the fried food was uniform in temperature and taste. The upgraded thermostat also ensured that the deep fryer would automatically turn off when the desired temperature was reached. John's deep fryer was a hit among home cooks, and soon he was manufacturing them for commercial use as well. Today, Hillier Fryers Ltd. is still a leading manufacturer of deep fryers, and its products are used by restaurants and households all over the world.
